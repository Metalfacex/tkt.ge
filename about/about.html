About Us

Connecting You to the Best Events, Entertainment, and Experiences in Georgia.

Our Mission

At TKT, we strive to provide an all-in-one platform for discovering, booking, and enjoying events across Georgia. Our mission is to make event access seamless and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone can easily connect with culture, entertainment, and experiences that inspire them. By simplifying event management and ticketing, we aim to build a vibrant community of event-goers and organizers.
